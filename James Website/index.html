Welcome to my Website!

My name is James Ho and thank you so much for stopping by my personal website.

The goal of this website is to share my experiences and perspectives with you, as well as to share my knowledge of this beautiful language known as chemistry. For those of you who have learned of this resource cite from either myself or recommended by others, please locate the links denoted "Chemistry Resources" and "Supplemental Instructions". It is my hope that you leave more enriched and having gained more expertise after visiting this website, whether it be in your academic careers or potentially as you begin the path onto your professional careers.

This is a non-profit website un-associated with Oregon State University. I will update the website periodically so make sure to be checking the website every week for new content. For those who will be tuning in frequently, whether it be for chemistry resources or to get an update on my life, I thank you for your presence during my journey and I hope you get a lot out of it.
