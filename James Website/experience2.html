Materials Discovery Lab (MaD Lab)

Spring 2020-Current

I work under Dr. Kyriakos Stylianou in his Materials Discovery Lab. In this lab, we specialize the use of Metal Organic Frameworks (MOFs) for the use of creating novel materials with unique functionalities applicable to real-world uses, such as gas capture, catalysis, photocatalysis, sensing and separatoin. I am indepentently working on my own project under the title: Enantioselective separation of chiral drugs using chiral Metal Organic Frameworks. I aim to use naturally occuring peptides and organic ligands to create these frameworks for separation large compounds on the basis of size, and later, on the basis of enantiomeric selectivity in racemic mixtures. Check out my research page for more info!
