Transport Phenomena and Thermodynamics

I was a learning assistant for the Chemical Engineering Transport Phenomena and Thermodynamics series at OSU. Included are review slides and worksheets I have created and answer keys along with them. There are also a collection of course notes, which I do not take credit for.
